Dairy Farming is a Biological System The dairy farm is dependent on the cow's ability to live a healthy life, produce milk, and have calves that can become the next generation of the farm. What is dairy farming in Uganda? Dairy farming is a strategic enterprise in Uganda that provides household food security and daily incomes to the large population.
